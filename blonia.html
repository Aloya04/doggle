Cracow's green playground

This lively meadow isn't just grass. It's a canvas of playfulness, where dogs chase dreams and families picnic with joy.

What can you do there?

  • Let your furry friends roam freely in the designated dog-friendly zones.
  • Spread a blanket and enjoy a leisurely picnic amid the lush greenery.
  • Participate in communal events, from yoga sessions to cultural festivals.
  • Find a quiet spot for relaxation or meditation amidst the serene atmosphere.
  • Connect with fellow visitors, share stories, and enjoy the sense of community.

History

Blonia, a green jewel nestled in the heart of Kraków, boasts a rich history dating back to the 19th century. Originally a military exercise ground, it has transformed into a sprawling meadow embraced by locals seeking a vibrant outdoor haven.
