Your gateway to a sporty lifestyle

Dive into a world where jogging trails and open fields beckon you to kick-start your active lifestyle. Don’t forget to bring your furry friend!

What can you do there?

  • Pack a picnic basket and relax under the shade of towering trees.
  • Unwind on park benches strategically placed for relaxation and reflection.
  • Wander through the park's botanical areas and appreciate the diverse plant life.
  • Discover sculptures and art installations scattered throughout the park.
  • Take a stroll or jog along the well-maintained walking paths.

History

Park Jordana, a verdant jewel in Kraków, traces its roots to the early 20th century. Originally designed as a botanical garden, it evolved into a beloved public park. Named after Ignacy Jan Paderewski, a renowned Polish statesman and artist, the park stands as a testament to the city's commitment to green spaces.
